Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 5, 2025

Welcome to Dentista Photo. We are committed to protecting your privacy and the privacy of your patients. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information that we receive through our dental photography application.

Information We Collect

Our app is designed to protect your privacy and that of your patients. Here's what we collect:

  • Google Account Information: When you sign in with your Google account, we receive basic profile information from Google.
  • Microsoft Account Information: When you sign in with your Microsoft account to use OneDrive integration, we receive basic profile information from Microsoft.

No Usage Data Collection

We do not collect any usage data, analytics, or tracking information from our users. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed our application to operate without monitoring how you use the app.

Data Storage and Security

Dentista Photo uses Your Cloud Storage of Choice as its primary storage solution. This means:

  • All patient photos and information are stored in your Your Cloud Storage of Choice account, not on our servers.
  • Your data remains under your control at all times.
  • The security of your data is primarily dependent on your Google account security.
  • We strongly recommend enabling two-factor authentication on your Google account.

Local Data Storage

The app stores some data locally on your device:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Temporary copies of photos before they are uploaded to Your Cloud Storage of Choice
  • Cached information for offline use

This local data is encrypted and secure on your device.

Patient Privacy Considerations

As a healthcare provider, you are responsible for:

  • Obtaining appropriate consent from patients before taking and storing photographs
  • Ensuring compliance with local healthcare privacy laws (such as HIPAA in the United States)
  • Maintaining the security of your device and Google account / Microsoft account
  • Using the app in accordance with your professional ethical obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information or patient data to third parties. Since we do not collect usage data or analytics, we do not share any data for analytics purposes.

Camera and Storage Permissions

Our app requires permission to:

  • Use your device's camera to capture dental photographs
  • Access device storage to save photos temporarily
  • Connect to your Your Cloud Storage of Choice account for data synchronization

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Acceptance of These Terms

By using Dentista Photo, you signify your acceptance of this policy. If you do not agree to this policy, please do not use our app. Your continued use of the app following the posting of changes to this policy will be deemed your acceptance of those changes.
