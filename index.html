Key Features

Designed specifically for dentists with the tools you need for professional intraoral photography

Specialized Dental Camera

Professional camera interface with guided photography and overlay frames for consistent, high-quality dental photos.

Intraoral & Extraoral Modes

Dedicated capture modes for I/O Upper, Lower, Right, Left, Front, and E/O Front, Left, Right views.

Patient Management

Organize patients by name and hospital number. Track visits and maintain comprehensive photo records.

Google Drive Integration

Seamless synchronization with Google Drive for automatic backup and access across devices.

Offline Capabilities

Work even without internet connection. Photos sync automatically when connection is restored.

Secure Data Storage

All patient data is securely stored in your own Google Drive account, giving you complete control.
